Guide

  • Add New Books: Easily add books to your collection with details like title, author, and publication year.
  • Manage Your Collection: View and manage your book details by clicking on its image in the library. In the detailed view, you can update or remove books by clicking on the 'Actions' button as your collection evolves.

    Note: To remove the current book cover image, there's an empty, non-editable field labeled 'Currently' at the end of the form. You'll find a checkbox there that you can tick to delete the cover.

  • Search: Quickly find books in your collection by title or author. Return to the library by clicking the 'Reset' button or by clicking the Logo or 'My Library' in the page menu.
  • Leave: Easily log out by clicking on 'Logout' in the page menu.
  • Animated line: If you wish, you can turn the line animation off by toggling the slider in the site menu.
