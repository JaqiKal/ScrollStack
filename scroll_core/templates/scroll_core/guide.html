Guide

  • Add New Books: Easily add books to your collection with details like title, author, and publication year.
  • Manage Your Collection: View and manage your book details by clicking on its image in the library. In the detailed view, you can update or remove books by clicking on the 'Actions' button as your collection evolves.

    Note: Ignore the empty, non-editable field labeled 'Currently' at the end of the form. This is due to a quirk in the template used.

  • Search: Quickly find books in your collection by title or author. Return to the library by clicking the 'Reset view' button or by clicking the Logo or 'My Library' in the page menu.
  • Leave: Easily log out by clicking on 'Logout' in the page menu.
  • Animated line: If you wish, you can turn animation off by toggling the slider in the site menu.
